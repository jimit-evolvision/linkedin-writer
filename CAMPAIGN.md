# AI Access LinkedIn Campaign Plan

**Product:** AI Access (Powered by Evolvision) — AI-powered real-time voice interview platform  
**Company:** Evolvision Technologies LLP  
**Author:** Jimit Joshi (CEO & Founder)  
**Campaign goal:** Build awareness among Indian hiring agencies. Onboard 10 pilot partners.  
**Pilot offer:** Free credits to screen 30 candidates. 1 feedback call per 10 screened. 10 spots max.  
**Target audience:** Founders and heads of recruitment/staffing agencies, HR Directors, Talent Acquisition leads at mid-size Indian companies running 10+ hires/month.

---

## Execution Rules

### Posting Cadence
- **3x/week:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday
- Consistency matters more than volume at 1,340 followers
- Never go silent for more than 5 days during the campaign

### Pilot Lead Tracking
- Pin the pilot post (Post 10) when it goes live
- Track "PILOT" comments manually — respond to every one within 24 hours
- DM follow-up: send a short intro + Calendly link or a one-pager about the pilot terms
- Keep a simple sheet: Agency name / Contact / Status (Commented / DMed / Call booked / Onboarded)

### Hashtags
Add after copy-pasting from the exported .txt file (do not include in the post body):
`#AIHiring` `#RecruitmentIndia` `#HRTech` `#TalentAcquisition` `#FutureOfWork`

### Paid Boost (Optional)
When Post 10 (direct pilot offer) goes live, consider a small LinkedIn Ads boost:
- Budget: ₹500-1,000
- Targeting: HR Directors, Recruitment Agency Founders, Talent Acquisition Heads in India
- Objective: Engagement (not clicks — LinkedIn's algorithm rewards engagement on boosted posts)

## What "Done" Looks Like

**Pilot phase complete when:**
- 10 agencies are running real hiring workflows through AI Access
- Each has completed at least one feedback call
- At least 3 have given a quotable reaction (even informal)

**Campaign success metrics:**
- 10 pilot partners onboarded
- 300+ candidates screened through pilot
- First paying customer converted post-pilot
- LinkedIn following growth to 2,000+ (organic, from campaign)
